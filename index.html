ğŸ“ How to Use

Upload Your Trading Journal

  1. Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar
  2. Upload a file (Excel/CSV) or Connect Google Sheets
  3. Map columns: Date, P&L, Charges (optional: Entry Type, Exit Criteria, Lots)
  4. View analytics instantly

Required Data Format

Your trading journal should have at minimum:

  • Date column: Trading date (DD-MM-YYYY or YYYY-MM-DD)
  • P&L column: Profit/Loss per trade
  • Charges column (optional): Brokerage fees, taxes

Google Sheets Integration

  1. Click "Connect Google Sheets"
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Enter your Sheet ID and Range (e.g., Sheet1!A1:E100)
  4. Authorize read-only access
  5. Your data syncs automatically
